Umbra

By Aidan Buffum and William Hartman

Umbra is an abstract puzzle game made as the final project for the course Digital Game Design I at Worcester Polytechnic Institute.

The player carefully navigates a ball to the goal while avoiding an encroaching darkness. The game is entirely browser friendly and played only with the arrow keys.

Programming by Aidan Buffum and William Hartman
Cover Art by Aidan Buffum

Contrast Bounce

By Aidan Buffum and William Hartman

Contrast Bounce is an abstract puzzle game made as a project for the course Digital Game Design I at Worcester Polytechnic Institute.

The player inverts the colors of the map to fire a ball at the goal. The game is entirely browser friendly and played only with the arrow keys.

Wizards Vs Robots

By Aidan Buffum, Matthew Kaminski, and William Lucca

Wizards Vs Robots is an action platformer game made as the final project for the course The Game Development Process at Worcester Polytechnic Institute.

The player controls a powerful wizard and takes him down one of two unique paths for a different experience during each playthrough. The player can switch between 3 different spells, each with their own effects and advantages to fight the evil robot horde in the wizard's way.

Programming by Aidan Buffum and Matthew Kaminski
Art by Aidan Buffum
Audio by William Lucca

Murder Bee Wrote

By Aidan Buffum, Kate Olguin, and Erik Cerini

Murder Bee Wrote is an infinite platform running game made during the Game Development Club at Worcester Polytechnic Institute's "Magic Realism Jam".

The player controls a murderer who has switched places with a bumblebee trying to sate his bloodlust against the bees all around him. Punch bees! Javelin them with the stingers of their fallen friends! Kill as many bees as possible to earn the ultimate high score!

Programming by Aidan Buffum and Erik Cerini
Art by Aidan Buffum and Kate Olguin
Audio by Kate Olguin
